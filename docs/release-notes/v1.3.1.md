# RoleLogic v1.3.1

_Released December 13, 2025_

---

## Bug Fixes

- **Fixed cross-guild role logging** — Cross-guild roles now display correctly in log messages.
- **Resolved race conditions & role spam** — Added a 1-second failsafe: after the bot adds or removes a role, that user is temporarily ignored for 1 second to prevent race conditions and spam-related issues.

---

## Improvements

- **Improved cross-action role accuracy** — Implemented double-checking for partial members or members with incomplete role caches to prevent missing data issues.
- **Increased revert threshold** — Revert limit increased from **10 to 100** to avoid unnecessary emergency stops during normal usage.
- **Improved UI role dropdown behavior** — After selecting another guild in the dropdown to load roles, the UI jumps to the first target role guild.

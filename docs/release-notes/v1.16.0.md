# RoleLogic v1.16.0

**Release Date:** July 17, 2026

---

## What's New

### Meet DWEEB — Design Discord Messages Visually

**DWEEB** is our free visual builder for Discord webhook messages, made by the RoleLogic team. Design announcements, welcome posts, and rich layouts — including buttons and Components V2 — with a pixel-accurate live preview of exactly how your message will look in Discord. It's free and requires no account.

You'll now find quick links to DWEEB on the RoleLogic homepage, in the dashboard sidebar (**Webhook Builder**), and in the footer.

### DWEEB Premium Is Included with Your RoleLogic Plan

RoleLogic and DWEEB now share **one premium system** — at no extra cost:

- Plans with **36+ slots** include **DWEEB Plus**
- Plans with **130+ slots** include **DWEEB Pro**

Your DWEEB tier is applied automatically to a server you choose, unlocking more scheduled posts, never-expiring panels, saved messages, live co-editors, and custom bots. The Upgrade page now shows exactly which DWEEB tier your plan includes.

### Live Setup Status Inside the DWEEB Editor

All 17 RoleLogic integrations — from YouTube, Twitch, and Steam to Birthday and Referral Code — now report a live **Ready / Needs setup** status inside the DWEEB editor when linked to your server, replacing the generic "set it up first" warning.

**Privacy note:** only whether the integration is set up (and its role count) is shared — never your configuration details.

### Forms: Pick a Form Right Inside DWEEB

Adding a form to a DWEEB message no longer means hand-copying links. Sign in once via a secure popup and pick any of your server's forms from a list, right inside the editor.

- Your sign-in is remembered for up to 12 hours, so it's roughly once a day.
- The editor's access is strictly **read-only** — it can list your form names and nothing more. All form management stays in your RoleLogic dashboard.

## Bug Fixes

### Kick Channel Role — Rebuilt on Kick's Official API

If you use the Kick integration, this is the headline fix. The integration has been rewritten against Kick's official API documentation, resolving an issue where channel events were never received — which meant subscriber and VIP rules could match no one.

- **Events now arrive reliably.** Follows, subscriptions, gifted subs, and channel activity from Kick are processed correctly.
- **VIP, Moderator, and OG badges sync automatically** from channel chat activity, and member roles update as badges change.
- **OG status handled correctly.** OG is now recognized as a per-channel badge granted by the broadcaster — not an account attribute. This adds a new **"My channel OGs"** rule preset, plus an OG badge and filter on the linked-users page.
- **Lapsed subscriptions expire gracefully** with a 2-day grace period, instead of depending on cancellation events Kick doesn't send.
- **No action needed.** Already-connected Kick channels repair themselves automatically — there's no need to reconnect.

# RoleLogic v1.6.1

_Released February 23, 2026_

---

## Patreon Account Linking

You can now **link your Patreon account directly** to RoleLogic. Instead of the old multi-step process (going to Patreon settings, connecting Discord manually, joining a server, then refreshing), you can now link your Patreon with a single click right from the dashboard.

### Two Ways to Activate Your Subscription

There are now **two methods** to connect your Patreon subscription to RoleLogic:

1. **Link Patreon to RoleLogic (Recommended)** — Click the "Link Patreon Account" button directly on the dashboard. This is the easiest method and takes priority over all other checks. Once linked, RoleLogic uses your linked Patreon account as the sole source for your subscription — no extra steps needed.

2. **Connect Discord to Patreon** — You can still connect your Discord account to Patreon through Patreon's social settings (Settings → Apps → Discord). RoleLogic will detect your subscription through this connection automatically. This method only works if your account is not already linked to RoleLogic.

### What's new

- A **"Link Patreon Account"** button on the Upgrade Plan page connects your account instantly
- The subscribe button now opens a convenient **popup window** — no more getting lost in new tabs
- Your subscription status **refreshes automatically** when you return to the dashboard (no more manual page refreshes)
- You can **unlink** your Patreon account at any time if needed
- Clear status messages tell you exactly where things stand — whether you're linked and active, linked but not yet subscribed, or need to link your account

---

## Faster & More Reliable Dashboard

- **Faster page loads** — the dashboard now caches your server permissions so repeat visits load instantly instead of re-checking every time
- **New loading screen** with friendly messages while things load, so you're never staring at a blank page

---

## Role Link User Limit Enforcement

- Role links that exceed the user limit will now **stop syncing entirely** until the user count is brought below the limit, rather than silently ignoring extra users. This makes it clearer when you need to upgrade or reduce users.

---

## Bug Fixes

- Improved reliability when verifying server manager permissions — if one method fails, the system now automatically falls back to an alternative check

---

## Language Updates

- Updated translations for Arabic, German, Spanish, French, Malay, and Portuguese (Brazil) to reflect all the new Patreon linking features
